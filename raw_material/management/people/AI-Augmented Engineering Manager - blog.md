# The AI-Augmented Engineering Manager: How I Run a Team in 2026

Source: Austin Xu's personal blog — distilled from direct eBay EM practice.

Core thesis: AI compresses the admin layer of EM work (~1/3 of previous time) so managers can invest in the irreplaceable human work — trust, judgment, relationships, difficult conversations.

## Six AI-Transformed EM Workflows (with outcomes)

| Workflow | Before | After |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Upward management (status updates) | 1–2 hrs/week | 15 min/week |
| Quarterly planning | ~1 month | 2 weeks |
| Incident / customer management | 3–4 incidents/month | ~1/month; 70% cases handled autonomously |
| Performance reviews | Memory-dependent | Evidence-based (NotebookLM + 1:1 notes) |
| Hiring pipeline | 3+ months, 3–4 hrs/interview | 4–6 weeks, ~90 min/interview |
| Remote onboarding | 3 months to productivity | 6 weeks |

## AI Handles vs. Manager Must Own

| Work Type | AI Handles | Manager Must Own |
|-----------|-----------|------------------|
| Documentation | Generate and structure | Decide what matters |
| Performance management | Gather evidence | Conduct actual conversations |
| Hiring | Screen and aggregate | Make final judgments |
| Project planning | Break down and track | Decide priorities |
| Technical direction | Research options | Choose direction |

## Critical Limitations — What AI Cannot Replace

- **Trust**: accumulated through consistency, follow-through, and genuine care — over time, in individual conversations. Cannot be manufactured.
- **Organizational judgment**: politics, unspoken priorities, cross-team dynamics — all require human relationships and contextual knowledge.
- **Crisis management**: during active incidents, pausing to prompt AI isn't viable. Preparation and calm judgment matter.
- **Curiosity**: AI amplifies existing curiosity but cannot generate it. Managers without natural inquiry won't benefit.

## Cautionary Lessons

- **Surface polish masks execution gaps**: beautiful documents don't guarantee understanding or alignment. Validate team comprehension through direct conversation.
- **Tool proliferation creates fatigue**: depth in 2–3 core workflows outpaces breadth across many tools.
- **Judgment atrophies without practice**: overreliance on AI for thinking degrades independent decision-making. Form your own view before consulting AI.

## The Differentiator

High-performing AI-augmented managers compress information management to invest in irreplaceable human work: relationship building, organizational navigation, technical credibility, and difficult conversations. Those who generate prettier documents without deeper impact fall behind.
